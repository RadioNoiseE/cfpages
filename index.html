Who am I?

I am Jing Huang, aka RnE or RadioNoiseE (web alias), a 12th grade IB student. This is my homepage. For a quick glance, click Memoir and Atom in the top navigation bar to explore my thoughts and the site’s update history.

About This Site

This site runs on clean HTML5, mostly transformed from XML via RsML, with a touch of handcrafted pages. This is a mirror site serving HTML5 instead of XML for compatibility. If you believe you have a browser with proper XSLT support, consider navigating to the main site for possible latest updates.

All links on this landing page are hidden! Hover your mouse over places where you think a link might be to reveal the underlined ones. Forged with Emacs nXML mode. An atom feed is maintained to help keep track of the site updates.
